About Me

Who I am: I am a serial volunteer and well into my career, I have exceptionally diverse experience ranging from programming for the web and mobile to milking cows and plucking apples as well as the distribution of foodstuffs. I graduated in 2004 with a generalist BFA and returned to school while working for the next 12 years taking STEM - mostly medicine (biology, anatomy, microbiology, chemistry), but computer science, as well. I am currently learning botany. I have invented numerous systems for the end consumer, farming, and medical use. These range from an EEG baby bonnet, to rope wicking water higher from an ever-dropping water-table, the engineering drawings of a pair of scissors designed to cut smooth muscle, and finally a new series of bone-screws to combat the inflating cost in medicine.
